Dylan // age 11 · SoCal

I live in Southern California, and besides 3D printing (my latest obsession) I love to skate, skimboard, play guitar and travel. My favorite subject is math, and my favorite place I've traveled to so far has been Spain.

Every item on this site is designed or hand-picked by me, then printed at home on my printer. In case you're wondering, my printer of choice is the Bambu Lab P1S.
